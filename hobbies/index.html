What are my hobbies?

As one might assume, as an ITM major at a tech university, I am a huge nerd. I collect Pokémon cards and Funko Pops with my girlfriend, Abigale. I also play my fair share of video games, athough I have not had as much freetime since this semester started. When I DO have enough free time, I like to play Old School RuneScape and Destiny 2. Once this pandemic is over, I would like to start playing Super Smash Bros. again with some friends. I work at Best Buy while also attending school full time, so I do not have as much freetime as I would like.

Collectibles

Pokémon Cards

Like many Americans, I found myself with a lot of spare time at the beginning of the Coronavirus pandemic. So, I started collecting Pokémon cards. While my collection is nothing special, it still is a lot of fun to rip open a pack or two after a long week. Since my girlfriend also likes to collect, it is really nice to find another thing for us to bond over. Our favorite sets to collect are: Vivid Voltage, Shining Fates, and the new Evolving Skies set that recently released this August.

Funko Pops

While Pokémon cards are a more recent collection, I have been collecting Funko Pops since 2016. I mainly collect the Dragon Ball line of figures, but I have branched out to other series like Full Metal Alchemist and Destiny. My girlfriend recently started collecting Hamilton Funko Pops as that is her favorite musical.

Gaming

Old School RuneScape

RuneScape is a game that almost feels like home to me. I have fond memories of playing it back when I was a child in grade school. Old School, with its 2007-esque aesthetic is very nostalgic to me. I enjoy playing "iron man mode" which turns the MMO into a single-player game, greatly increasing the difficulty.

Destiny 2

Destiny is... a very special game to me. Sure I've played video games for hours on end, staying up way too late, but never have I been so enthralled by a video game franchise. It is my favorite game. I have met so many friends on that game and even run a clan with two other close friends I made playing Destiny. I have four exclusive jackets for completing a new raid im the first week of release. I am very passionate about the game.
